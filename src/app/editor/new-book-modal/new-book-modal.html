Statements is a convergence of word processing and messaging enabling rapid drafting and improvement of documents.
Authors and reviewers have different roles and editing privileges in Statements. Authors have full control over all content within the document being drafted, while reviewers are capable only of commenting upon sentences within the document. Both roles have abilities to message.
Interactions in Statements take place primarily at the sentence level. To interact with a sentence of message, follow the "click, type, enter" model. The last step commits a sentence ot the document and simultaneously sends the sentence out as a message.
